Async Corporation

The Async Corporation is a research institute that originates from the Frontrooms. Their entry into the Backrooms was made possible through their KV-31 Project, which allowed them to pierce the veil into what they refer to as "The Abridged Reality".

While their goals remain unclear, it is believed that Async is utilizing the Backrooms as a storage and housing facility. Their outposts have grown significantly in recent months. The methods they used to enter remain undisclosed.

If you encounter them, use caution. In some cases, trades are possible — they show interest in gold, silver, and placeable light sources. However, provocation may lead to confrontation.

Known Ranks within Async

  • High Ranks: These include founders and top executives overseeing the KV-31 project and other classified operations.
  • Corporate Security (CS): Tasked with defending Async outposts and escorting research and supply teams. They wear distinct blue uniforms.
  • Corporate Analytics (CA): Researchers and analysts who study the Backrooms. They wear purple uniforms similar to security uniforms.
  • Corporate Logistics (CL): Responsible for delivering supplies from the Frontrooms through the KV-31 checkpoint. They wear green uniforms.
  • Interns: Believed to be newer recruits or technicians, possibly handling computing or monitoring tasks.

Relations with Wanderers

Async operatives can be neutral or hostile depending on your behavior. Offering resources they consider valuable may result in trade or cooperation. However, repeated intrusion into their territory may provoke defensive responses.

Outposts and Activity

Async has established outpost in Level 0. CS patrols have been observed guarding these areas. Internal operations remain tightly controlled and access is forbidden to outsiders without permission.
