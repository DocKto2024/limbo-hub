Level ❖ - "Abyss"

Survival Difficulty: Class 4 — Unsafe | Very rare resources presence | Alot of enigmatic life-forms

Description

Level ❖, commonly known as "The Abyss" or "Brick level" within the survivor community, is a vast, shifting labyrinth made entirely of brick. Illumination is minimal, provided only by scattered, eerie street lamps that emit dim, flickering light, heightening the claustrophobic and foreboding atmosphere.

The level's layout changes periodically, which makes navigation and mapping difficult. The oppressive silence is broken only by strange ambient noises that seem to follow the explorer, causing immense mental strain.

Resources

  • Firearms and Ammunition: Rare but very useful.
  • Gold Bars: Common metal on this level, can be used to trade but dont worth much.
  • Pale Keys: Extremely rare, may be used to exit the level.
  • Glowsticks: Can be found in chests.
  • Royal Rations: A luxury meal, very expensive and rare.

Threats

  • Hostile entities: Enigmatics can be found here.
  • Danger level architecture: High risk of death from falling.
  • Hostile survivors/groups: Bandit groups and other hostile persons can be meet here.
