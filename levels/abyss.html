Level ❖ - "Abyss"

Survival Difficulty: Class 4 — Unsafe | Very rare resources presence | Alot of enigmatic life-forms

Description

Level ❖, commonly known as "The Abyss" or "Brick level" within the survivor community, is a vast, shifting labyrinth made entirely of brick. Lampposts can be found every where.

The level's layout changes periodically, which makes navigation and mapping difficult.

Resources

  • Firearms and Ammunition: Rare but very useful.
  • Gold Bars: Common metal on this level, can be used to trade but dont worth much.
  • Pale Keys: Extremely rare, may be used to exit the level.
  • Glowsticks: Can be found in chests.
  • Royal Rations: A luxury meal, very expensive and rare.

Threats

  • Hostile entities: Enigmatics can be found here.
  • Danger level architecture: High risk of death from falling.

Exits

Lots of noclip walls exist throughout the maze. Some may return you to earlier levels some to another part of maze.

Passing through bright white portal lead to Olympus.

Additional Notes

Its difficult to navigate due to level size, using of maps reccomended.
