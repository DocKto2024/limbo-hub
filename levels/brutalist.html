Level ⟁ - "Brutalist"

Survival Difficulty: Class 2 — Unsafe | Minimal Resources | Environmental Hazards

Description

Level ⟁, known as "Brutalist", is a maze-like space consisting of dimly lit concrete corridors with brutalist-style architecture. The lighting is extremely poor, so survivors must carry light sources before attempting to traverse. The maze is eerily quiet, with distant echoes resonating throughout the halls.

Resources

This level provides minimal resources. Food and light sources cannot be found here. Survivors may encounter melee weapons such as fire axes, shovels, and sledgehammers scattered across the level.

Threats

  • Hazardous Architecture: Unstable structures are common. Survivors must tread carefully to avoid fatal accidents.
  • Entities: Butchers and enigmatic entities wander this level, posing a severe threat.
  • Other Survivors: Hostile survivors may also be encountered.

Exits

The level contains several noclip-based exit routes requiring precise sequences:

  • Noclip Sequence: Locate a wall marked with "1/2". Noclip into the wall to access a room with three noclip points: a pillar, a tunnel, and another wall.
  • Sequence tunnel → pillar → wall → pillar → tunnel ➝ Leads to a Brutalist Parkour.
  • Sequence tunnel → pillar → wall → pillar → pillar → tunnel ➝ Returns to Motel Depot.
  • After the sequence tunnel → pillar → wall, a small crack in one of the walls can be found. ➝ Crawling through it leads to Concrete Town.

Additional Notes

  • A flashlight is mandatory for traversing due to the pervasive darkness.
  • Traveling in groups is highly recommended for safety and navigation.
  • The unstable architecture poses serious risks — move cautiously and remain vigilant.
