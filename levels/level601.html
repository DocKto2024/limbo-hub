Level 601 - "The End"

Survival Difficulty: Class 1 — Safe | Not Many Resources | No Entity presence

Description

Level 601, also known as "The End", is a small level which resembles a library. The level is small, but features in the level are the following; gramaphones on the tables, books scattered around the place, bunch of book shelves, tables/chairs, and the well known "The End" text on the wall.

Level 601 has two typewriters with weird numbers coming out of it. 1 typewriter has dots and lines while the other is composed of 0's and 1's.

Resources
