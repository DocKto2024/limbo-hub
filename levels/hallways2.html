Level H — "Hallways?"

Did i go to a wrong noclip?

Survival Difficulty: Class 3 — Mostly Safe | Moderate Resources | Hostile Entity Presence

Description

Level H appears to be a massive abandoned mental hospital, with different areas including offices, showers, dining halls, archives, storage rooms, and more.

Posters and signs written using "Local language" can be found throughout the facility. The structure itself appears to be part of a "Megastructure"

The level have a big amount of "Computers", our people use it to acces our data base.

Threats

  • Entities: Mannequins, Ghosts, and Orblings are frequently encountered.
  • Other Survivors: Hostile individuals remain a constant danger.

Entrances and Exits

  • Noclipping through the floor in one of the offices (where a chair can be seen stuck halfway through the floor) will lead back to Level 0.
  • Noclipping through a wall at the end of a long corridor of interconnected offices leads to Level Brutalist.

Additional Notes

More information is required to fully document Level. Exploration is considered moderately safe, though explorers are advised to remain vigilant due to hostile entity activity.

[FERG Field Log] — “Posters suggest an possible organized past, but the hospital is completely abandoned. The mannequins lurking the halls, and we have no clue of the anomaly. Recommend caution.”
