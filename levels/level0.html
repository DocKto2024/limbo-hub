Level 0 - "The Infinite Hallways"

Survival Difficulty: Class 3 — Moderately Safe | Many Resources | Entity Presence

Description

Level 0, also known as "The Infinite Hallways", is the starting point and central hub of the Backrooms. It consists of endless yellow-tinted corridors featuring stained yellow wallpaper, damp carpet, and the persistent buzz of fluorescent lighting. The monotony and lack of variation cause disorientation and psychological stress.

The level expands infinitely in all directions with similar rooms and hallways. Variants such as wall scratches, stains, and rare structural anomalies occur, but the overall layout remains eerily consistent.

Resources

  • Food: Food bars, bottled water, and various other consumables can be found.
  • Weapons: Monkey wrenches, knives, and spears may be located in scattered areas.
  • Light Sources: Placeable lamps and flashlights are commonly found, useful for navigation.

Resources are rarely clustered and typically appear at random locations.

Threats

  • Entities: Bacteria-based creatures roam the hallways. Especially dangerous for lone survivors.
  • Hostile Survivors: Violent individuals may attack others near scarce resources.
  • ASYNC Operatives: While usually passive, ASYNC staff may become hostile if provoked.
  • Environment: Long-term exposure to the air may be harmful due to toxic airborne elements.

Exits

Exits are difficult to find due to the uniform nature of the level. Known exits include:

  • KV-31: Leads to a more industrial, hazardous environment.
  • Level 0.1, 0.2, 0.3 and other: Accessible through elevators, doors, noclips; each features unique layouts.

Additional Notes

ASYNC documentation suggests Level 0 contains between 9-10 vertical floors. Exploration of vertical shafts or hidden access panels may lead to undocumented sublevels.

Breathing the air for prolonged periods may lead to nausea, hallucinations, or respiratory issues due to potential contamination.

Survivors should avoid prolonged isolation and always travel equipped with light and water.
