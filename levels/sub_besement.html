Level ⌘ - "Sub Besement"

Survival Difficulty: Class 4 — Unsafe | Very rare resources presence | Enigmatic life-forms present

Description

Level ⌘, commonly known as "INFORMATION OMMITED" or "Sub Besement" within the survivor community, is a level with very chaotic infrastructure. Mannequins can be found around the level. The only known way get here is using pale key on level Abyss.

Piano room

In the center of the room there is a piano with roots growing out of the ceiling playing a melody on the piano, the melody of the piano can be heard throughout the entire level.

Several chairs are placed around the room, there is also one door behind which there is a no-clip, passing through the no-clip leads to the main part of the level.

Paintings room

Not far from the exit, you can find a room covered with sheets of something similar to paper or fabric, with various patterns and symbols drawn on them, it is unknown who or what drew them and what they mean.

Resources

  • Strange gear: Strange gas mask and strange chestplate can be found here.

Threats

  • Hostile entities: Some of the enigmatics can be found here.

Exits

Goin through a no-clip lead back to Abyss.

Additional Notes

High risk high reward area.
