Piano room

In the center of the room there is a piano with roots growing out of the ceiling playing a melody on the piano, the melody of the piano can be heard throughout the entire level.

Several chairs are placed around the room, there is also one door behind which there is a no-clip, passing through the no-clip leads to the main part of the level.

Paintings room

Not far from the exit, you can find a room covered with sheets of something similar to paper or fabric, with various patterns and symbols drawn on them, it is unknown who or what drew them and what they mean.
