Limbo Language

Description

The Limbo Language, also known as the Anacrite Symbol System, is a mysterious written form found throughout various levels of Limbo. It appears to be a constructed adaptation of the English alphabet, transformed into complex symbolic runes.

Each character corresponds to one or more letters of the Latin alphabet.

Structure and Usage

  • One symbol can represent one or two letters depending on the mark placement.
  • The language includes unique marks for sentence start, exclamation, period, comma, and apostrophe.
  • Some letters may be combined or slightly modified without losing their meaning.

Examples

BAD STORM. BIG WINDS.

Translation of a documented inscription:

“Bad storm. Big winds.”

Alphabet

A-A. B-B. C-C. D-D. E-E. F-F. G-G. H-H. I-I. K-K. L-L. M-M. N-N. O-O. P-P. R-R. S-S. T-T. U-U. W-W. Y-Y. Z-Z.

Notes

  • Believed to originate from unknown entities, real owners/creators of the backrooms or ancient civilization.
  • Some versions differ slightly between regions, suggesting local evolution of the script.
  • The symbols known as Limbo language since people love it as a unique way to communicate beetween limbobians.
