Pale Key

Description

The Pale Key is a rare and mysterious object that occasionally appears throughout various levels of the Backrooms.

When used, the Pale Key is inserted directly into the floor by the player, triggering an immediate noclip event that sends the user to either an unidentified level or a random location somewhere in the Backrooms.

Its behavior is unpredictable, but it has the unusual capability of transporting individuals to unique levels that are otherwise unreachable, such as Level-363 and the Pillar level.

Documented Locations of Appearance

  • Brutalist
  • Brick
  • Motel Depot
  • Possibly other levels (unconfirmed)

Notes

  • Appearance rate: very low
  • Once used, it disappears permanently
  • Possibly can lead to new undetified levels
  • The destination cannot be chosen or predicted
