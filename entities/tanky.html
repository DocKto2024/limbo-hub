Tanky

Community Nicknames: Tanky

Description

Tanky is a larger variant of a normal Blanky. It has six hand-like feet and a massive tail ending in three strange “fingers.” Despite its intimidating size, it is not naturally hostile toward survivors.

Behavior

Tanky is non-aggressive, primarily focusing on hostile entities rather than humans. It is generally friendly with survivors and will not attack unless provoked. Most of the time, Tanky roams across levels searching for food. Survivors have reported that Tanky can alert them of danger by slamming its tail against the floor, producing a loud, metallic sound that serves as a warning.

Countermeasures

Tanky poses no direct threat to survivors. Combat is unnecessary and discouraged, as Tanky actively avoids harming humans. However, if provoked, it can easily kill. The best survival strategy is simple: leave it alone.

Moveset

  • Tail Spin: Spins its tail around its body, dealing high damage.
  • Charge Forward: Charges toward a target with devastating impact.
  • Eating: Can take food from survivors, even directly from their hands.

Statistics

  • Lethality: High (but only when provoked)
  • Mobility: Normally slow, similar to a Blanky; capable of running but rarely does so.
  • Vitality: Very high — Tanky’s natural armor protects it from most damage.

Known Locations

  • Found wandering across most levels.

Notes

  • Death Message: “Elder Lifeform.”
  • Please avoid harming Tanky. Survivors report that it can actively defend humans against hostile entities.
