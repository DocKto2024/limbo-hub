Abilities

  • Sword Mastery (Muramasa): Skilled and fast attacks; heals on kill.
  • Relentless Pursuit: Chases targets with unyielding determination.
  • Mocking Laughter: Instills fear; can disorient survivors during chases.
  • Lantern Bludgeon: Secondary weapon; can deliver devastating melee blows.

Countermeasures

  • Noclip Escape: Most effective method of avoiding death.
  • Avoid Open Levels: Don’t engage it in areas with no cover or escape routes.
  • Coordinated Assault: Only way to kill it reliably. Avoid casualties that could trigger its healing.

Statistics

  • Lethality: Extremely High
  • Mobility: Fast and relentless
  • Vitality: High (regenerates from kills)

Known Locations

  • Level 0 Sub-Regions: Extremely Rare sightings. There has only been 1 report on a Butcher being in Motel Depot.
  • Brutalist Level: Frequent and dangerous encounters
  • Brick Level: Frequent and dangerous encounters

Notes
