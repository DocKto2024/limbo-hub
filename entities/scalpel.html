Scalpel

Community Nicknames: Scalpel, Unevolved Dissector

Danger Level: Moderate

Description

Scalpels are considered the less developed, unevolved form of the Dissector species. They share a similar humanoid structure but are weaker and less imposing, with wiry limbs and blades that are less refined compared to their evolved counterparts.

Their bladed appendages are shorter but still sharp enough to deal significant damage. Like the Dissectors, they possess erratic movement patterns and varying head shapes, including some with horn-like protrusions.

Behavior

Scalpels are aggressive but less calculated than Dissectors. Once a Scalpel has spotted a player, it will chase them relentlessly.

A key behavior of the Scalpel is its tendency to lunge at targets. This lunge is powerful, but if it misses and hits a wall, the Scalpel will be temporarily stunned, giving players an opportunity to escape or attack.

General Information

  • Can make a short forward rush.
  • If it hits a wall during a lunge, it becomes stunned for a few seconds.
  • Additional data to be filled out.

Countermeasures

  • Dodge its lunges to force it into walls, stunning it momentarily.
  • Avoid tight, open spaces where it can quickly close the distance.
  • Utilize its predictable lunging behavior to set traps or ambushes.
  • Make sharp turns or find cover.
  • Hide in the dark when possible.
