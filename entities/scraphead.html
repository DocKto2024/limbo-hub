Scraphead

Community Nicknames: Scraphead, Pipehead

Description

Scrapheads are aggressive, armless entities slightly taller than the average player. Their bodies are riddled with pipes, stabilized by bacterial wires forming their internal structure. Their most notable feature is a circular head with a large pipe embedded inside. Despite lacking arms, they are extremely dangerous, relying on their powerful legs and head for lethal attacks.

They can fit through tight, player-sized spaces, giving them an edge in cramped levels..

Occasionally, Scrapheads may accidentally provoke other bacteria-based creatures by attacking them, leading to internal fights among entities.

Behavior

Scrapheads are unrelenting predators. They do not retreat once locked onto a target, continuing their assault until death. While blind, they possess extremely acute hearing, able to track players in complete darkness purely through sound. Light does not affect their behavior, making sound discipline critical to avoid detection.

Countermeasures

  • Stop moving immediately if one is nearby.
  • Create distance before attempting to hide. Then remain silent.
  • Use dark areas to mask your presence from other entities.
  • Make sudden, sharp turns to escape during a chase.
  • Crouch walk to reduce noise.
  • Trigger alarms or sound traps to mislead them.
