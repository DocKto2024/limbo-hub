Dissector

Community Nicknames: Butcher

Danger Level: High

Description

The Butcher is a formidable and terrifying entity, distinguished by its menacing appearance and relentless pursuit of survivors. It is clad in a large, well-fitted jacket that comes in a range of colors—blue, green, or red. In one hand, The Butcher wields its signature weapon: a massive bloodstained sword, known as Muramasa, wrapped in ominous black cables. In its other hand, or sometimes at its hip, The Butcher carries a large lantern, which it also uses as a brutal melee weapon. Its sinister laugh echoes in the Backrooms whenever it corners or surprises a target, making encounters with this entity deeply unnerving.

Behavior

Dissectors are highly hostile and engage immediately upon sight. Their movements are quick and calculated, often employing hit-and-run tactics. However, they will disengage if they lose sight of their target, making them unpredictable.

Countermeasures

  • Hide in the shadows to avoid detection.
  • Use confined spaces to limit its movement.
  • Take sharp turns to confuse and outrun it.
  • Coordinate with others to flank and attack simultaneously.
  • Wait for it to make an attack and then escape during the brief pause.

Moveset

  • Slash: A fast, sharp attack dealing significant damage. Can kill an unarmored survivor in just a few hits.
  • Lunge: A sudden leap forward, attacking with both scythe-like appendages.
  • Crouch: Can crouch to fit through smaller spaces, but unable to attack while in this position.

Statistics

  • Lethality: 33-70 damage per attack, depending on the move.
  • Mobility: Faster than the average survivor.
  • Vitality: Moderate health, can withstand several hits before going down.

Locations

Primarily found on Level 0 and areas with higher bacterial activity, such as the Nest.
